4D Recovery Adolescent Transportation

Ensuring accessibility for all participants through structured transportation services.

🚐 Transportation Services

4D Recovery offers transportation to its adolescent clients to ensure they can access the recovery center during structured pick-up and drop-off windows. These services are provided on a first-come, first-serve basis, ensuring equitable access for all participants.

Outside of these specified windows, mentors may depart from the center for 1:1 appointments to meet participant needs.

📅 Daily Transportation Planning

Each day, 4D Recovery Service staff meet at 12:30 PM to coordinate transportation. Due to high demand, there may be capacity limitations. In such cases, 4D Recovery will communicate with clients and guardians to arrange alternative transportation solutions.

🛑 A return ride home will be provided for those picked up by 4D Recovery staff. If return ride capacity becomes an issue, staff will contact the client’s guardian for assistance or provide updated drop-off times.

📝 General Pick-Ups

On designated General Pick-Up days, 4D staff commit to providing transportation for clients. Participants who need a ride must complete the Ride Request Form by 11:00 AM on General Pick-Up days to allow for coordination.

❗ On Tuesdays and Thursdays, 4D Recovery Adolescent staff are unable to provide general pick-ups due to capacity constraints.
