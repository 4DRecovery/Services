🛤️ Our Two Service Tracks – Working Together for the Best Outcomes

At Young Recovery Adolescent Services, we believe that the most successful path to long-term recovery includes:

  • ✔ Clinical treatment to address mental health and substance use challenges with professional support.
  • ✔ Peer-based recovery support to help youth stay engaged, build connections, and develop leadership skills.
  • ✔ Active family involvement to create a strong foundation for sustained healing and growth.

When these services are combined, adolescents receive a comprehensive and holistic recovery experience—one that treats the underlying issues, builds a supportive recovery network, and strengthens family connections to promote long-term success.

By engaging in both Clinical and Recovery Support Services, youth benefit from:

  • ✔ Increased stability – Professional therapy and structured peer support work together to build a strong recovery foundation.
  • ✔ Stronger social networks – Positive peer connections and mentorship help reinforce healthy behaviors.
  • ✔ Better long-term success rates – Research shows that youth who engage in both treatment and ongoing recovery support experience higher rates of sustained recovery.
  • ✔ Empowered families – When caregivers are actively involved, youth have a stronger support system and better outcomes.

Our dual-track approach ensures that youth are not only receiving clinical care but also developing the peer and family support needed to thrive in recovery.

✨ Recovery Support Services

Our peer-based recovery support services provide a welcoming space for youth seeking long-term recovery and personal growth.

At 4D Recovery, we believe that recovery should be engaging, empowering, and sustainable. Our Recovery Support Services aim to:

  • ✔ Make Recovery Fun: We create a safe and inclusive space where young people can build friendships, engage in activities, and experience joy in recovery.
  • ✔ Increase Youth Agency & Leadership: We empower youth to take an active role in their recovery by providing leadership development opportunities and decision-making roles in peer-led initiatives.
  • ✔ Develop Ongoing Social Support: We help youth cultivate a strong network of peers, mentors, and community members who provide encouragement, accountability, and long-term support.
  • ✔ Build Recovery Capital: We equip youth with the personal, social, and community resources necessary to achieve and sustain recovery, including life skills, employment readiness, and education support.
  • ✔ Encourage Individualized Pathways: We recognize that recovery is unique to each individual. Our services support diverse recovery pathways, whether through 12-step program, spiritual growth, or other evidence-based models.

By fostering connection, leadership, and resilience, our Recovery Support Services help young people navigate their journey toward a meaningful, self-directed, and substance-free life.

📝 Community Agreements

All participants engaging in Recovery Support Services at the community center are expected to uphold these agreements:

  • ✔ Respect: Treat all individuals with dignity and respect, fostering an atmosphere of inclusivity, support, and recovery.
  • ✔ Abstinence: Commit to maintaining sobriety and refraining from the use of substances during participation in center activities.
  • ✔ Accountability: Take responsibility for your actions and their impact on the community.
  • ✔ Confidentiality: Respect the confidentiality of fellow participants and the information shared within the center, including obtaining permission before taking or sharing photos of individuals.
  • ✔ Contribution: Contribute positively to the community through active participation and engagement in activities.
  • ✔ Clean up after yourself: Maintain cleanliness and orderliness within the community by cleaning up after yourself.
  • ✔ Boundaries: Each person has their own boundaries; at 4D Recovery, I will set my own and respect others.
  • ✔ Safety: No weapons, stealing, hate speech, fighting, sexual conduct, or drug use.
  • ✔ Nicotine Free: Tobacco use is not permitted on the premises or within 4D Recovery Vehicles.
